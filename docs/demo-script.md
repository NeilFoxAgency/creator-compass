# Three-minute demo script

**0:00–0:20 — Problem**  
Brands know creators matter, but databases give them thousands of names before answering the more important question: where does this brand belong?

**0:20–0:45 — One input**  
Enter a brand website. CreatorCompass reads a bounded set of public pages and shows real analysis stages—no account and no fake progress percentage.

**0:45–1:25 — Territory map**  
Reveal three Core territories, two Adjacent opportunities, one Experimental direction, and two Risk zones. Open a territory to show audience connection, creator profile, campaign concepts, objection, and supporting evidence.

**1:25–1:55 — North Star and readiness**  
Show the recommended territory, campaign format, creator-size direction, test shape, and fix-first list. Expand two readiness dimensions.

**1:55–2:20 — Transparency and resilience**  
Open “How CreatorCompass decided.” Explain that deterministic code owns the scores; facts, inference, assumptions, and unknowns remain distinct; blocked sites can continue from pasted text.

**2:20–2:45 — Codex and GPT-5.6**  
Codex built and iterated on ingestion, scoring, provider routing, UI, tests, and Cloudflare deployment. Neil made the product, ethical, scoring, and design decisions. GPT-5.6 is the final strategic reviewer: it rejects weak candidates, resolves the portfolio, and chooses the North Star from evidence-backed options.

**2:45–3:00 — Impact**  
CreatorCompass does not bury brands in creator data. It gives them a direction they can understand, test, and act on. End on the report and Neil Fox Agency attribution.
